Starfire für Clash Royale

Die perfekte ergänzungs App für Clash Royale, seht detaillierte Statistiken für euch, den Clan, chatfunktion und mehr

Starfire is the ultimate companion app for Clash Royale.

Starfire features:

• Automatic stat and chest cycle tracking
• Clan stats and recruiting
• Real-time chat channels - share text, images, and gifs
• Private messaging
• Push notifications - stay up to date with chats, announcements, and events

...and much, much more! Starfire is constantly evolving based on community feedback!
